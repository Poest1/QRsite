Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a business card?

Tap the "Scan" button on the home screen, point your camera at a business card, and hold steady. The app will automatically detect and capture the card, then extract all the contact information using AI.

How does iCloud sync work?

When enabled, your business cards automatically sync across all your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account. If you reinstall the app, your cards will be restored automatically.

Can I export cards to my Contacts app?

Yes! Open any saved card, tap the share button, and select "Save to Contacts". You can also export as a vCard file to share via email or message.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud account. We never send your data to external servers, and we don't use any analytics or tracking. Read our Privacy Policy

The OCR didn't recognize something correctly. What should I do?

You can edit any field by tapping on it in the review screen. For best results, ensure good lighting and hold the camera steady. Cards with unusual fonts or designs may require manual correction.

How do I set up follow-up reminders?

After scanning a card, enable "Follow-up reminder" in the review screen. Choose when you want to be reminded, and the app will send you a notification at that time.

Can I share my digital card via QR code?

Yes! Go to "My Card" tab, set up your digital business card, then tap "Share QR Code". Others can scan this code to instantly save your contact information.

  • Make sure you have an internet connection
  • Try force-closing and reopening the app
