Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

CardScan Pro AI is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on external servers. All your business card data stays on your device and your private iCloud account.

1. Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any data to external servers

2. Data Storage

All business card data you scan and save is stored:

  • Locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • In your private iCloud account (if you enable iCloud sync)

Your data is encrypted by Apple's security frameworks and protected by your device passcode and iCloud account credentials.

3. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud synchronization:

  • Your business cards are synced across your Apple devices
  • Data is stored in your private iCloud container
  • Only you have access to this data
  • Apple's end-to-end encryption protects your data
  • We cannot access your iCloud data

You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings.

4. Camera & Photo Library Access

The app requests camera access to scan business cards. Photos are:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never uploaded to any server
  • Stored locally only if you save the card

5. Contacts Access

If you choose to export a business card to your Contacts app:

  • The export happens entirely on your device
  • No contact data is sent anywhere
  • You control which cards to export

6. Notifications

If you enable follow-up reminders:

  • Notifications are scheduled locally on your device
  • No notification data is sent to external servers

7. Third-Party Services

CardScan Pro AI does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's iCloud for optional data synchronization.

8. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete individual cards: Swipe to delete in the app
  • Delete all data: Uninstall the app
  • Delete iCloud data: Manage in iOS Settings → Your Name → iCloud → Manage Storage

9. Children's Privacy

CardScan Pro AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
